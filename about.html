About Jalev

JALEV is a website dedicated to the promotion of Latinx artists of all mediums. The art world is already so comptetitive and hard to stand out in; here we make it easier for them to reach an audience that is most likely to understand them and their expierence in life. Our hope is to also encourage more people from Latin descent to partake in artistic expressions, by showing them these succesful artists.
