Featured Artists


Miguel Luciano

Miguel Luciano is a Puerto Rican multimedia visual artist based in New York, whose work explores themes of history, popular culture, and social justice through sculpture, painting and socially engaged public art projects.


Joiri Minaya

Joiri Minaya is an American multidisciplinary artist of Dominican descent based in New York; she works with digital media, photography, film, performance, sculpture, textiles and painting.


Raúl de Nieves

Raul de Nieves is a Mexican multimedia artist, performer, and musician, based in Brooklyn, New York.


Candida Alvarez

Candida Alvarez is an American painter of Puerto Rican descent, and a tenured professor of Painting and Drawing at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago where she has taught since 1998.
